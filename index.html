Our Tiramisu Story

Founded in 2020, Tiramisu Box brings authentic Italian dessert experience to your home. Our master chef, trained in Venice, uses only the finest ingredients - imported Italian mascarpone, freshly brewed espresso, and delicate ladyfinger biscuits.

Each tiramisu is carefully prepared 24 hours before delivery to ensure perfect texture and flavor development. We deliver fresh daily to guarantee you receive the best possible product.

Order before 2pm for same-day delivery in the city area.
